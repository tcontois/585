Attempt at combining POS sequence with 3-gram word sequences.
Took a part of speech sequence from an actual Obama speech
Then looked at the intersection of words from Obama speeches with the correct POS tag intersected with words from the n-gram model

My thoughts:
Not that great. Seem less coherent than normal n-gram markov. Don't really need POS in picking word because n-gram markov already picks words that make sense, i.e. it picks from a list of words that have actually followed to previous 2 words in an Obama speech.
Topic biasing also didn't work as well because more focused on POS tag than topic file.
